The crypto/ directory isolates the SDK's pure cryptographic operations, mathematical verification, and threshold schemes from the domain logic. It is divided into five distinct areas:1. crypto/hash.go (Canonicalization)Purpose: The root hashing primitive for the SDK.Context: Based on our Phase C locked plan, this file is the designated home for the LengthPrefixed TupleHash function. It enforces the universal rule that all fields (and the DST) must be length-prefixed before hashing to prevent boundary-shifting collisions.2. crypto/artifact/ (Proxy Re-Encryption)Purpose: Handles the threshold cryptography for sealing and selective access to artifacts.Key Files: * pre.go: Implements the Umbral Proxy Re-Encryption (PRE) scheme. This contains the logic for generating KFrags, re-encrypting CFrags, and executing the dual-verification gates (DLEQ proofs + Pedersen polynomial consistency).api.go: Exposes the public artifact encryption and decryption interfaces.3. crypto/escrow/ (Key Recovery & Custody)Purpose: Handles splitting, routing, and recovering master keys (e.g., for user identity recovery).Key Files:vss_v2.go: Implements the Pedersen Verifiable Secret Sharing (VSS) logic and the derivation of the ComputeEscrowSplitID.verify_share.go: Contains the validation logic (validateShareFormatV1 and V2) to ensure shares match the published commitments.blind_routing.go & ecies.go: Handles the ECIES-wrapping of plaintext shares so they can be securely routed to proxy nodes without being exposed on the log.4. crypto/signatures/ (Identity & Proof Verification)Purpose: Routes and verifies digital signatures across different cryptographic curves and standards.Key Files:entry_verify.go: The main cryptographic gate that verifies the signature on every Envelope submitted to the log.bls_verifier.go & bls_signer.go: Implements BLS12-381 signatures, which includes the explicit RFC 9380 Hash-to-Curve IETF carveout we discussed (exempting it from the LengthPrefixed rule).eip191.go & eip712.go: Implements Ethereum-compatible signature verification, allowing web3 wallets (like MetaMask) to sign log entries.witness_verify.go: Handles the $K$-of-$N$ aggregate signature verification for the decentralized witness nodes (verifying tree heads).5. crypto/admission/ (Ingress Stamping)Purpose: Handles cryptographic operations at the log operator's admission gate.Key Files:stamp.go & adapter.go: Likely manages the generation and verification of admission stamps (cryptographic receipts proving when an entry was accepted by the operator before SMT inclusion).
